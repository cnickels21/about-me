Meet the Real Me!

Hello! I was born in Colorado, but at two years old my family moved to northern Wisconsin; specifically a small town on Lake Superior. Because of this I have always had a natural calling to have both a large body of water and mountains in my life. I grew up playing saxophone, but over the years have learned to play guitar, bass, and piano. I currently play bass in a band called Them Dirty Bones. We have one album and one national tour under our belts, and we are in the process of writing our second album.

My parents own a bar and grill as well as a motel back home; currently in their 27th year of business! This, combined with some unfinished secondary education, has given me a decent background in business management and accounting. I have worked in the service industry for most of my life either cooking or bartending. I also have some experience with construction through my dad's independent contracting business.

I am happy with what I have accomplished in my life thus far, but I know there is a lot left for me to do. I have a natrual wander lust and want to see what the rest of the world has to offer me. More specifically, I feel that there is something for me to go experience in Germany. I am hoping that through DeltaV, and the skillset they can give me, I will be able to achieve this goal of seeing the world through technological innovation.
